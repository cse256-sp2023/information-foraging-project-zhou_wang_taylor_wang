After Admission

After successfully admitted to Washington University Nursery School, parents are welcome to check the Parent Association and school merchandise under Get Involved!


* Important Note: You need to pay one month of tuition as a deposit through mail or online. More information can be found under Tuition on the menu on the right.

Half-day Session Deposit: $500

Full-day Session Deposit: $1105


Enrollment Form

The Enrollment Form needs to be filled out every year that your child attends WUNS. Please filled out this form online after admission. This link can also be found under Forms. Fill out the Enrollment Form


Developmental History Form

The Developmental History Form needs to be filled out after being admitted into WUNS. This form only needs to be filled out once. This link can also be found under Forms. Fill out the Developmental History Form


Additional Forms

The two medical forms (below) are only available as PDFs and must also be filled out and returned.
